Academic Projects

Guaranteed Social Campaign Based on Targeted Advertising, Dec 2018

Viacom want to offer Guaranteed Social Campaign to their advertisers. For example, Viacom want to guarantee their advertisers that their ads will reach a certain number of views (eg. 1000 impressions) over a certain period of time (eg. a week). Viacom want to charge advertisers $25 per 1000 impressions. This project is to find out whether Guaranteed Social Campaign could be profitable for Viacom. (slides)

Skills: data cleaning, data exploration, data analysis, R, Tableau
